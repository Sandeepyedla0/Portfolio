AI & ML Timelines — Evolution & Impact

Visual timeline of key AI milestones from early logic-based intelligence to modern Generative AI.

  • 1️⃣ Turing Test – A test where an evaluator tries to tell apart a human and a machine; if they can’t, the machine is considered intelligent.
  • 2️⃣ ELIZA Chatbot – Used pattern matching and prewritten responses to simulate human conversation — the first step toward modern chatbots.
  • 3️⃣ 1977 Milestone – Machines began processing data, calculating moves, and making logical decisions, outperforming humans in specific tasks.
  • 4️⃣ Neural Networks & Vision – Neural networks revolutionized computer vision by recognizing images more accurately than ever before.
  • 5️⃣ Cloud & Generative AI – Platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud powered the rise of Generative AI models such as GPT-3.
  • 6️⃣ Quantum AI Future – Quantum computing could make AI 13,000× faster, supercharging intelligence and solving problems beyond today’s limits.
